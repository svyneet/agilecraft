Smarter Library Management System

This application helps you to keep track of and maintain the various transactions taking place in the library.It is a system having two leves of previliges, i.e Student and Admin. It enables the “admin” to create and delete users (student and staff). He can edit his profile, change password, enter purchase details of books or delete them, view the profiles of the students as well as the staff, check if a book is available or not, verify whether a book is in stock or not, view details and status of book, fine details etc. The staff members can also edit his profile, change password, issue books to students, view the profiles of the students, check if a book is available or not, verify whether a book is in stock or not, view details and status of book, fine details etc. Similarly, the student can view and edit his profile, change password, check if a book is available or not, check his/her status regarding how many books are issued/returned on which date, and the fine levied correspondingly. An amicable interface and a robust database creates a user-friendly environment, so that the library records can be maintained easily and efficiently, and a secure login panel restricts unwanted interference.

High School Accounts Management System

Pearl Academy is a highly reputed high school in Guwahati,India. But they were tired of their manual accounts management system. So,they wanted a simple accounts management system to register new students, collect tution and other types of fees and print out Fee reciepts and have the data stored. We developed a custom Admin Panel based web application with a our custom backend framework to serve there requirements.

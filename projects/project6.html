AK Survey

The survey initiative will perform a research project using an online medium in which participants will be subjected to a quiz, instrument and an intervention. Results will be collected via the online portal and will be provided to the researchers in an anonymous format. The site will contain the provision for users/participants to be tested in groups and group access can be limited by time intervals.
